Data Collection

The data we collect are, your username that you enter when creating your account, your password and the statistics of each completed game. The collected statistics are composed of:

  • Final points
  • Accuracy
  • Number of errors
  • Longest sequence of words without mistakes in a row
  • Total number of words written during the game
  • Total number of failed words
  • Date in seconds of the end of the game
  • Time spent on the game

    • Data processing

    Usernames and statistics are processed by us on our server and by other players to be displayed in the ranking, for the rest, i.e. passwords, they are only stored on our server.
